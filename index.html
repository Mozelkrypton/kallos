Who We Are

A generation rising with purpose

Kallos Youth Empowerment (KYE) is a Christian, youth-focused nonprofit committed to the holistic empowerment of young people aged 18–30. We build disciples, develop leaders, and ignite a Kingdom culture through discipleship, mentorship, and practical initiatives.

What We Do

Three pillars.
One generation.

Every KYE initiative flows from three interlocking commitments — because lasting transformation is always spiritual, social, and economic at once.

✦

Spiritual

Discipleship programs, Bible study fellowships, prayer movements, and worship gatherings that root identity in Christ.

◎

Social

Mentorship, mental health support, and community service missions that build resilient, connected young people.

◈

Economic

Skills training, entrepreneurship support, career development, and table banking to create sustainable livelihoods.
