What to Expect

Types of gatherings

Weekly

Small Group & Bible Study

Intimate discipleship groups meeting weekly or biweekly — the relational core of KYE where real growth happens.

Monthly

Fellowship Gatherings

Larger community meetings for worship, teaching, prayer, and celebration of what God is doing in the community.

Quarterly

Outreach Missions

Community service and mission initiatives that take the work of KYE beyond our walls into the neighborhoods that need it most.

Annual

Leadership Summit

An intensive annual gathering for leaders across all levels — vision-casting, training, and strategic planning for the year ahead.

Skills

Workshops & Training

Practical sessions on entrepreneurship, career development, financial literacy, and vocational skills tied to the economic arm.

Special

Prayer & Worship Nights

Dedicated evenings of corporate prayer, intercession, and worship — anchoring the entire movement in God's presence and purpose.

Article 12.3 — Safe Events

Every event is a safe space

All KYE events undergo risk assessments, require appropriate safeguarding coverage, and follow our health & safety plans. Consent is obtained for all photography and media. No participant is ever coerced into spiritual activities as a condition of attending.
