Article 6 — Membership

There's a place
for you here

KYE does not discriminate based on tribe, ethnicity, gender, disability, or socio-economic status. Whatever season you're in, there's a way to belong.

Article 6.1 — Categories

Three ways to belong

01 — General Member

Join as a Member

For young people aged 18–30 who want to participate in KYE programs, discipleship, and community life.

  • Access to spiritual, social & economic programs
  • Mentorship & small group community
  • Leadership growth pathway over time
Apply as a Member
02 — Volunteer / Partner

Volunteer or Partner With Us

For mentors, trainers, churches, NGOs, and businesses who want to support KYE's mission with time, skill, or resources.

  • Written volunteer agreement & clear scope
  • Supervision, recognition & support
  • Documented time commitment — no open-ended asks
Volunteer or Partner
03 — Core Team Leader

Apply for Leadership

For those called to lead — appointed leaders responsible for strategy and execution across KYE's departments and programs.

  • Merit-based, values-aligned recruitment
  • Mandatory safeguarding & ethics training
  • Clear role description & reporting line
Apply for Leadership

What happens after you apply

01

Application

03

Get Started

Tell us about you

Fill in the form and a member of our team will follow up with next steps based on the track you select.

General membership is for ages 18–30. Volunteers/partners may be outside this range.

Our standard for everyone

Per Article 10.6, KYE holds zero tolerance for harassment, exploitation, abuse, fraud, or corruption — for staff, volunteers, and members alike. Integrity, respect, and professionalism are non-negotiable.
