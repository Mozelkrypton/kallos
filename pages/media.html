Articles 14 & 15 — Brand & Data Use

How we handle media & data

All KYE-branded content requires approval for co-branding and consistent use of our identity. We obtain documented consent before publishing images or testimonials, and process personal data lawfully, fairly, and with appropriate security safeguards. Spokespersons for KYE are limited to the Director, Board Chair, or delegated leaders.
